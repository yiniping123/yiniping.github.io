Research Experience

The problem of path decomposition for graphs with treewidth at most 4 (April 2020 - November 2020)
Advisor: Prof. Changhong Lu, ECNU

  • The renowned Gallai Conjecture posits that a connected simple graph with n vertices can be decomposed using at most ⌈n/2⌉ paths. My master's thesis expanded on Botler's findings, demonstrating that the conjecture also holds true for graphs with treewidth at most 4. (Accepted by Discrete Mathematics, First Author and Corresponding Author) [Paper]

Optimizing Integer Division: A Study on the Unsigned Newton-Raphson Method (June 2023)
